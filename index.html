About PolyTrack

PolyTrack is an exhilarating low-poly racing game that brings the excitement of high-speed racing to your browser. Inspired by TrackMania, it features challenging tracks with loops, jumps, and intense racing action where every millisecond counts.

With its built-in level editor, you can create your own tracks and share them with the community. Race against the clock, master challenging obstacles, and compete for the best times on various tracks.

  • Low-poly graphics style
  • High-speed racing action
  • Built-in level editor
  • Time trial racing
  • Track sharing system
  • Challenging obstacles
