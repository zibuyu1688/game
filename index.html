About Plants vs. Zombies

Plants vs. Zombies is the classic tower defense game that has captured the hearts of millions. Defend your home from invading zombies by strategically placing different types of plants with unique abilities.

Collect sunlight, plant your defenses, and use each plant's special abilities to stop the zombie invasion. With various plant types and zombie enemies, every level brings new challenges and strategies.

  • Diverse plant types
  • Strategic gameplay
  • Multiple zombie types
  • Sunlight collection system
  • Progressive difficulty
